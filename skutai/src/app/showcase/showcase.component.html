SV Sports Internal Portal
While working as a full-stack software developer at SV Sports, I helped build the internal portal. Other employees used the portal to manage the company's inventory, make requests to the production team, and view relevant data about company activities. Some of the parts I worked on helped create item data, purchase orders, and audit invoices. I mainly worked with Angular and .NET while building the portal.
Fake Cats
The mission of this assignment was to generate fake cat photos. The project was done with Tensorflow in Python. The method used to produce the photos was a convolutional Neural Network. The network can accept many similar images and then create new versions of the same thing that could fool a human.
Bag of Words
For this AI assignment, I used logistic regression to train a model that can determine whether a review is positive or negative. With enough information, the model becomes very accurate. The algorithm is transparent as well. That is, many useful pieces of information can easily be pulled out of it. For example, we can view how positive or negative a specific word is according to the system.
Video Amplification
If you point a camera at a person trying to sit still, the camera will pick up on small motions like the person's breathing and pulse. In this project, my RUE team and I set out to capture and amplify such motion so that a person can view what would otherwise be invisible to them. We also used a depth camera to amplify motion in higher dimensions. Use the link to the right to see the paper that inspired the project.
Ray Tracer
I used GLSL and JavaScript to add raytracing to a scene in this assignment. The ray tracer works within a browser and runs in real time. It allows for different effects to be visible, such as the reflections of light in mirrors, the shininess of objects, and the shadows cast when a light ray is blocked. To build up the ray tracer, I was required to complete several tasks like adding Blinn-Phong lighting, finding the closest surface to the camera that a ray hit, and calculating the distortions of stretched or rotated objects.
Polygon Mesh Processing
For this, I needed to implement a list of mesh processing methods to view details about various half-edge meshes. The methods were programmed using JavaScript. The finished project allows a user to highlight the edges related to a face or a point. It could also tell the area and normal of a face. Along with this, one can inflate, deflate, smooth, and sharpen a 3D mesh as much as they want. The image to the left shows off the normal of the points of the mesh (the purple lines), the edges related to one of the points (the blue lines), and the edges to the mesh that are associated with a null face (the green line).

Smoke Stack
A muralist repainted an old smokestack at Ursinus College. She added many glyphs to the surface, representing meaningful things about the school and its community. In this project, I was given and also took some images and videos of the smokestack. Then, I created a 3D model of the smokestack using the media and some photogrammetry software. After that, I made the model available online for the muralist to annotate. You can now view the project's final product online by clicking on the link to the side.
Safari Man
This was a project from when I studied human-computer interactions. My other group members and I used Python to make an interactive app that takes speech as input. The program allows users to log in using a username and password or use neither and continue as a guest. After that, users can interact with the program in a number of ways and also have their computer do things like open Google Chrome to search for things online. When finished, a person can say “goodbye,” and the program will close itself.
Location Note
Location Note is the name I gave to the app I made in my human-computer interactions class. It is an Android app that allows users to save a note to an address. Then, when the person visits that location, they will get a notification with the note that they wrote. The app also saves the notes people have made so they can view them at any time.
